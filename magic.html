Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are there stories. We also need to stress that whateveer happens to you as a result of consuming Magic Cake is your responsibility. You've been warned, and you can view our legal page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  • Mix eggs,sugar, and butter in a large bowl
  • Spread into non-stick dish
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  • Let sit at rom temperature for 20 minutes
  • Eat and enjoy!
